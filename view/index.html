Facility policies

BOOKINGRULES.

  1. Weekly LimitA user may book a maximum of 10 hours per week per sport.
  2. Daily LimitA user may book a maximum of 4 hours per day per sport.
  3. Payment RequiredAll confirmed bookings must be paid for as per facility guidelines.
  4. Non-Payment = BlacklistingUnpaid bookings may result in account suspension or permanent blacklisting.
  5. No-Show PolicyRepeated no-shows may lead to booking restrictions or account suspension.
  6. Cancellation WindowCancellations and reschedules are allowed up to 6 hours before the booking time.
  7. Booking VerificationPlayers must present their booking confirmation or verification code at check-in.
  8. Play at Your Own RiskThe facility is not responsible for injuries, accidents, loss, theft, or damage.
  9. Respect the FacilityMisconduct, abuse, or damage to property may result in immediate removal and suspension.
  10. Accurate Details OnlyUsers must provide valid contact information when making bookings.
  11. Time Slot ComplianceCourts must be vacated at the end of the booked slot unless an extension is approved.
  12. Management Rights ReservedThe facility may cancel bookings or suspend accounts in cases of misuse, policy violations, or operational requirements.
