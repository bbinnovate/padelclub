How do I pay?

Book your slot, then scan the UPI QR on your confirmation. The front desk updates your payment status.

How do I choose my duration?

Tap consecutive 30-minute slots on the same court or ground. Select at least two slots to make the one-hour minimum.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes, up to 6 hours before your booking, subject to court availability.
